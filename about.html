About Katz ACE Hardware

A Fixture in Glastonbury for Generations

Katz ACE Hardware has been proudly serving the Glastonbury community for generations. As your locally owned and operated hardware store, we are committed to providing exceptional customer service and a wide selection of quality products to meet all your home improvement needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to be the most helpful hardware store on the planet. We believe in treating every customer like a neighbor, providing expert advice, and carrying the products you need to get the job done right.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Local Expertise: Our knowledgeable staff lives and works in this community and understands your needs.
  • Personal Service: We take the time to help you find exactly what you need.
  • Quality Products: We carry trusted brands like Benjamin Moore, Weber, Scotts, EGO, Traeger, and YETI.
  • Convenient Services: From key cutting to paint matching, propane filling to glass cutting—we do it all.

Visit Us Today

Stop by our store at 2687 Main Street, Glastonbury, CT and experience the difference of shopping at a true neighborhood hardware store. Our friendly staff is ready to help you with all your hardware, paint, lawn and garden, and home improvement needs.
